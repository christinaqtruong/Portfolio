About Me

Born in the city of Round Rock, I studied at the University of Texas where I finished a B.S in Microbiology and a B.S in Medical Laboratory Science.

I completed my 16-month clinical program in the DFW area, where I got my first job as a ASCP-certified MLS at Parkland Memorial Hospital. After working in the Chemistry department for about a year, I moved to Austin and began working nights as a generalist for Baylor Scott & White Medical Center in Pflugerville. As a generalist I ran the entire laboratory alone at night, covering the chemistry, blood bank, urinalysis, immunology, body fluids, and hematology departments working 76-hour weeks.

I have been working on independent projects and recently completed the Web Development Coding Bootcamp at the University of Texas at Austin.
